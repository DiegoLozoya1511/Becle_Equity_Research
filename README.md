## Investment Thesis

The recommendation rests on three structural pillars:

**1. Vertical Integration as an Unreplicable Competitive Moat**  
Becle owns the largest Blue Agave plantation in Mexico within the legally protected Denomination of Origin region. This converts agave price cycles from an industry-wide risk into a sustained cost advantage. While peers absorbed the same input cost volatility, Becle delivered 280 bps of gross margin expansion in 2024, a benefit that non-integrated competitors structurally cannot replicate.

**2. Pure-Play Leadership in the Fastest-Growing Spirits Category**  
Within a decelerating global distilleries industry (0.93% growth in 2024), tequila has compounded at 7.1% annually in the U.S. since 2003. With 28.8% global market share by volume and 73% of revenues derived from tequila, Becle is the undisputed pure-play leader. The deliberate divestiture of b:oost and a super-premium tier already representing 48.2% of revenues position revenue-per-unit expansion as the dominant value creation mechanism going forward.

**3. Deleveraging and FCF Recovery Reveal a Discounted Valuation**  
Free cash flow swung from MXN -5,844M in 2022 to MXN +6,088M in 2025, driven by CAPEX discipline as major infrastructure investments neared completion. Net Debt/EBITDA collapsed from 2.88x in 2023 to 0.88x in 2025, the lowest among global spirits peers. Against this backdrop, a current valuation of 6.23x EV/EBITDA represents a material discount we expect the market to correct as earnings quality and FCF durability become increasingly evident.

## Valuation Methodology

The target price of **MXN 21.71** is derived from a weighted average of two DCF approaches:

- **FCFF (80% weight):** Intrinsic value MXN 23.38. Measures firm-level cash generation independent of financing decisions, making it the structurally robust anchor.
- **FCFE (20% weight):** Intrinsic value MXN 15.00. Captures the scenario in which systematic debt repayment of MXN 2,000M per year materially affects equity-level cash flows.
